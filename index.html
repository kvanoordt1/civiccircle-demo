For Precinct Delegates

CivicCircle gives Ottawa County precinct delegates the tools to actually reach their residents — a civic calendar, issue reporting, community posts, and a direct line to local officials. Free for every citizen. Built for the delegate who wants to do the job right.

No password. No commitment. Admin-verified before activation.
What is a precinct delegate?
You're elected by your neighbors to represent them in your local party organization. Most delegates don't realize what that actually means — or what tools they're missing.
🏘
The most local elected seat
A precinct delegate represents just a few hundred neighbors — closer than any township board member, county commissioner, or state legislator.
📣
Real access to officials
Township boards answer their phone. County commissioners show up to meetings. State legislators track constituent contact by district. You have standing. Use it.
🔗
The missing link
Most citizens don't know who their delegate is. Most delegates have no way to reach their residents. CivicCircle is the connection layer that should have always existed.
⚡
Ottawa County has 121 precincts
Most are unactivated. Every delegate who joins CivicCircle gives dozens of residents a civic home. Ottawa County. Spring Lake. Your neighborhood.
What CivicCircle gives you
📅
Complete civic calendar
Every public meeting from your township board through the Michigan Legislature. Your residents always know what's happening and when.
🚧
Structured issue reporting
Residents report civic concerns to you, not directly to overloaded officials. You triage, route to the right person, and track resolution.
🏘
Community Bridge
Post announcements, town hall notices, and neighborhood updates. Your residents react and engage. No social media noise.
📬
Weekly email digest
Every Tuesday, every enrolled resident gets a brief, plain-language summary of what's happening in their precinct. You send it. CivicCircle formats it.
🔑
Personal enrollment codes
You hand codes to residents in person. That face-to-face moment is the trust relationship. CivicCircle doesn't replace it — it gives it a front door.
